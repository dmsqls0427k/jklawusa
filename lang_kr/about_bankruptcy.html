Immigrant Visa

The available categories which the Law Office of Jong K. Lee is prepared to assist clients are:

 

FAMILY SPONSORED IMMIGRATION

Persons who qualify are Immediate relatives of a sponsoring individual, they are: Spouses of United States Citizens; unmarried children under the age of 21; and parents of United States Citizen.

There are 4 preferences (categories of individuals)

First Preference – Unmarried children o

Second Preference – Spouses and children of Legal Permanent Resident

Third Preference – Married Children of United States Citizen

Fourth Preference – Siblings of an adult United States Citizen

EMPLOYMENT BASED IMMIGRATION (EB-)

EB-1 - First Preference – Priority Workers (extraordinary ability)

EB-2 - Second Preference – Professionals with Advanced Degrees

EB-3 - Third Preference – Skilled Workers – Professional and Other

EB-4 - Fourth Preference – Special Immigrant Religious Worker

EB-5 - Fifth Preference – Employment Creation
